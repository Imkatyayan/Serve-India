service grant model — helping the deprived

The project delivers fund to the talented people residing in rural areas deprived from quality education and opportunities due to poor financial background. This is a universal donation platform bridging the gap between the poor and rich sections of the society

Donor

People willing to help someone can register as donor on the platform and donate funds to the talented people deprived from opportunities due to lack of aid. The donor can help such people in attainment of their goals and to pave a path for a better tommorrow.

Receiver

The people registering themselves as Receiver, seek help for quality education and opportunities so as to pave their path to success. Receiver will be directly geeting the funds into their bank account without the involvement of a middle-man.

working

The donors can browse throught the receiver and shortlist the receivers matching their crateria and can transfer funds to their respective accounts. This will result in the direct transfer of funds in betwween the donor and the receiver, thus eradicating the chain of transfer causing corruption.

benefits

The project provides transparency of fund transfer which results in lowering corruption across the nation. Avoiding involvement of any middle-man and any other organization, thus providing better and secure future for the generation.

Our Vision

Our goal is to connect needful rural people to people who are willing to help without a middle-man.where anyone can log on and look for help and anyone can log on and donate. The person donating the fund will browse through the web application filtering the authenticated receivers based on his/her preferences to fund the short listed candidates provided by this platform. In this platform people can register and serve the talented people in the rural areas and provide them with funds and scholarships paving their path to success and later help other unprivileged and talented people through the same platform.
